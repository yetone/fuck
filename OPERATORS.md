# Operators

| Arithmetic operators  | Syntax
| :------------         | :----
| Addition              | a + b
| Subtraction           | a - b
| Multiplication        | a * b
| Division              | a / b
| Modulo                | a % b

| Comparison/relational operators   | Syntax
| :------------                     | :---- 
| Equal to                          | a == b
| Not equal to                      | a != b
| More than                         | a > b 
| Less than                         | a < b 
| More than or equal                | a >= b
| Less than or equal                | a <= b
